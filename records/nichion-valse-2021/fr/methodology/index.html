Evidence Classification

DOCUMENTÉ

Primary source directly supports the statement.

CORROBORÉ

Multiple independent records support the statement.

DÉCLARATION D'UNE PARTIE

A statement made by one party but not independently established.

INFÉRÉ

A reasoned inference from the records, clearly marked as such.

CONTESTÉ

The parties or records provide conflicting interpretations.

QUESTION OUVERTE

The reviewed materials do not resolve the issue.

CONTEXTE SEUL

Public background information that does not establish record-specific conduct.

QUESTION OUVERTE
QUESTION OUVERTE

Meaning of a missing document

Absence of a located document does not prove that no such document ever existed. It means only that no such document was identified in the reviewed corpus.

Quotes and originals

Confidentialité et protection des sources

La correspondance sous-jacente n'est pas publiée intégralement. Les données personnelles, informations de tiers et détails identifiants de communication sont retenus. Les résumés publics sont limités aux informations nécessaires pour expliquer la structure transactionnelle documentée.
